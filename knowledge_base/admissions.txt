 Murang’a University of Technology (MUT) admits students through multiple intakes in January, May, and September. 🍂
Admission requires:
- A KUCCPS placement letter (for Kenyan students)
- Application form (downloadable or from Registrar’s office)  
- Non-refundable fee: KSh 500 (Craft), KSh 1,000 (Diploma), KSh 1,500 (Degree), KSh 2,000 (Postgraduate) :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
